Obituary: Thomas P. Miller

It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Thomas P. Miller, a beloved member of our community. Born in Syracuse, New York, Thomas resided in Lowville, New York, where he made a lasting impact on the lives of those around him. On December 23, 2023, at the age of 72, Thomas left us, leaving behind a void that will be deeply felt by his family and friends.

Early Life and Residence

Thomas P. Miller’s journey began in Syracuse, New York, where he was born and raised. Growing up in this vibrant city, he developed a strong sense of community and a passion for making a difference. Later in life, Thomas made Lowville, New York, his home. This picturesque town became the backdrop for his cherished memories and the place where he touched the lives of many.

Passing and Mourning

The news of Thomas P. Miller’s passing has left a profound impact on all who knew him. As we mourn the loss of this remarkable individual, we find solace in the memories we shared and the legacy he leaves behind. Thomas was a loving husband, father, and grandfather, and his absence will be deeply felt by his family. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Cheryl D. Farr, his children, and his grandchildren, who will undoubtedly miss his presence dearly.

In this time of grief, we invite you to join us in honoring Thomas’s memory by sharing your condolences on the memorial page dedicated to him. Your kind words and support will provide comfort to his family during this difficult period.

Family and Predeceased Members

Thomas P. Miller was a cherished member of a loving family, and his passing leaves a void that will be deeply felt by those closest to him. Throughout his life, Thomas experienced both the joys and sorrows of family, and his journey was shaped by the relationships he held dear.

Surviving Family Members

Thomas is survived by his beloved wife, Cheryl D. Farr, who stood by his side through thick and thin. Their enduring love and partnership were a source of strength and support for both of them. In addition to his wife, Thomas leaves behind a legacy of love through his children. David Miller, Roxanne Miller-Woods, Christopher Donaldson, and Scott Farr were blessed to call him their father. Thomas’s guidance, wisdom, and unwavering love will forever be cherished by his children.

Grandchildren and Great-Grandchildren

Thomas P. Miller’s legacy extends beyond his immediate family to the next generations. He was a proud grandfather to Shayla, Megan, Axton, Peyton, Willow, Ariana, Nicholas, Hailey, and Liam. These grandchildren were the light of his life, and he cherished every moment spent with them. Thomas was also blessed with great-grandchildren, Connor and Case, who brought him immense joy and happiness. His love and influence will continue to live on through these young lives.

Visitation and Funeral Details

As we gather to honor the life of Thomas P. Miller, we invite you to join us for the visitation and funeral service. These events provide an opportunity for us to come together as a community and pay our respects to a remarkable individual who touched the lives of many.

Funeral Service

We encourage all who knew Thomas to attend and pay their final respects.
