Solar Opposites Season 4 Cast List: Who Stars In The New Season?

Publish date: 2024-07-05

The animated comedy series Solar Opposites is back for a fourth season on Hulu. The show follows a family of aliens from another planet who are forced to relocate to Earth. The family consists of Korvo, a scientist who hates Earth; Terry, a muscle-headed meathead who loves Earth; Yumyulack, a genius who loves to shrink people and put them in his terrarium; and Jesse, a teenage girl who is just trying to figure things out.

The cast of Solar Opposites Season 4 remains largely the same as the previous seasons. Justin Roiland voices Korvo, Thomas Middleditch voices Terry, Sean Giambrone voices Yumyulack, and Mary Mack voices Jesse. Sagan McMahan voices The Pupa, the mysterious alien creature that the family is protecting.

Solar Opposites Season 4 Cast List

Solar Opposites Season 4 will also feature some new guest stars. Dan Stevens will voice a new character named Korvo Prime, an evil version of Korvo from the alternate dimension. Christina Hendricks will voice Cherie, a human who befriends the aliens. Tiffany Haddish will voice Aisha, a rival alien who is also trying to take over Earth.

Here is a more detailed look at the cast of Solar Opposites Season 4:

Justin Roiland Portraying Korvo

Justin Roiland as Korvo: Korvo is the leader of the alien family. He is a brilliant scientist, but he is also very cynical and pessimistic. He hates Earth and wants to leave as soon as possible.

Thomas Middleditch Portrays Terry

Thomas Middleditch as Terry: Terry is Korvo’s partner and the muscle of the family. He is a bit of a meathead, but he is also very loyal and caring. He loves Earth and wants to make a life for himself there.

Sean Giambrone Portraying Yumyulack

Sean Giambrone as Yumyulack: Yumyulack is Korvo and Terry’s replicant son. He is a genius, but he is also very sadistic. He loves to shrink people and put them in his terrarium, which he calls “The Wall.”

Mary Mack As Jesse

Mary Mack as Jesse: Jesse is Korvo and Terry’s human daughter. She is a teenager who is just trying to figure things out. She is often caught in the middle of her parents’ conflicts and their alien adventures.

Sagan McMahan Portrays The Pupa

Sagan McMahan as The Pupa: The Pupa is a mysterious alien creature that the family is protecting. It is said to be the future of their species, but its true purpose is unknown.

Dan Stevens

Dan Stevens as Korvo Prime: Korvo Prime is an evil version of Korvo from the alternate dimension. He is more ruthless and ambitious than Korvo, and he is determined to take over Earth.

Christina Hendricks As Cherie

Christina Hendricks as Cherie: Cherie is a human who befriends the aliens. She is a single mother who is struggling to make ends meet. She finds comfort in the aliens, who are also outsiders.

Tiffany Haddish Portrays Aisha

Tiffany Haddish as Aisha: Aisha is a rival alien who is also trying to take over Earth. She is more cunning and manipulative than the other aliens, and she will stop at nothing to achieve her goals.

Solar Opposites Season 4 is sure to be a wild and hilarious ride. With a great cast and a new batch of guest stars, the show is sure to entertain fans of all ages.
