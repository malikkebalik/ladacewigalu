ATLANTA, GEORGIA: The deep-rooted friendship between Kandi Burruss and Rasheeda Frost has stood the test of time, transcending their respective reality TV shows, 'RHOA' and 'Love and HipHop'. Their connection runs so deep that they even joined forces as collaborators under the pseudonym Peach Candy, leaving an indelible mark in the entertainment world.

In a heartwarming gesture, Kandi recently shared a special birthday video to commemorate Rasheeda's milestone celebration on May 25. As Rasheeda turned 47 years old, Kandi's video not only melted the hearts of fans who admired their friendship, but it also evoked a wave of nostalgia. What stood out, in particular, was Kandi's choice of song, which added an extra layer of sentimental value to the birthday tribute, resonating deeply with the viewers and sparking a flood of cherished memories.
