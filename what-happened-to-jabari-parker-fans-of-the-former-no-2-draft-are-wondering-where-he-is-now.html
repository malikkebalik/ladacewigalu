What Happened To Jabari Parker? Fans Of The Former No.2 Draft Are Wondering Where He Is Now

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Jabari Ali Parker is a professional basketball player from the United States who most recently played for the NBA's Boston Celtics (NBA). The Milwaukee Bucks selected him with the second overall choice in the 2014 NBA draft.

He has been released by the Boston Celtics. Parker has been considered a draft flop owing to his lack of success in the NBA, despite being one of the most decorated high school basketball stars in American history.

What Happened To Jabari Parker?

Jabari Ali Parker has failed to live up to the hype in the NBA since his stealer college career. The Celtics let him go because they liked having an open roster slot, but it was also an economic choice.

After an MRI confirmed a torn anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) in his left knee on February 9, Parker was declared out for the remainder of the 2016–17 season.

In April 2021, Parker joined the Boston Celtics. During the 2020-21 campaign, he participated in 10 games for the team, averaging 6.4 points, 3.6 rebounds, and 1.0 assists in 13.8 minutes, according to the Bleacher Report.
