Pat Warren, an Anchor and Political Reporter at WJZ-TV | CBS Baltimore, has passed away RDCN

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Pat Warren, a cherished anchor and longtime political reporter at WJZ-TV | CBS Baltimore, has left us over the weekend. The community mourns the loss of this respected journalist, known for her enduring presence and insightful political reporting.

Pat Warren’s journalistic journey began in 1992, when she joined WJZ-TV and CBS Baltimore. Over the years, she established herself as a respected anchor and reporter, earning a reputation for her smart and thorough reporting. Pat’s reporting style was characterised by a unique blend of accuracy and conciseness, coupled with a great sense of humour that endeared her to audiences. Her career spanned nearly three decades, during which she became an integral part of the newsroom and a familiar face to viewers.

The sudden and unexpected demise of Pat has left the TV|CBS Baltimore viewer community in mourning. According to a close family member, Pat experienced a brief medical issue that tragically led to her passing. The news was shared by Mike Hellgren of WJZ CBS Baltimore on Facebook, initiating an outpouring of condolences and memories from the community.

In a heartfelt message on Facebook, Bailey Chiquita conveyed deep sorrow and shared a news alert about the passing of Warren.

News Alert: So very sorry to share the news WJZ-TV | CBS Baltimore received over the week-end that long-time anchor/reporter Pat Warren has passed. She was one of the top traffic reports there for over 30 years. RIP @PatWarrenMedia_
