How did Scott Darrel Fossum Die? Snowmobile Incident Explained

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Scott Darrel Fossum, a beloved husband, and father of two sadly passed away on January 22nd, 2023 at the age of 34. Detroit Lakes Newspapers was the one who first broke the news of this tragic accident via Facebook and shocked the whole community as well as left many questioning how such a devastating accident could have occurred.

In this article, we will explore the circumstances surrounding Scott’s death and provide a detailed explanation of the snowmobile incident that ultimately led to his passing.

What Happened to Scott Darrel Fossum? Snowmobile Accident Explained

Scott D. Fossum was born on February 25, 1988, in Park River, North Dakota. He was an experienced snowmobiler, having grown up in a small town in Minnesota where the winter sport is a popular pastime. On the day of the incident, he had decided to take his snowmobile out for a ride on a well-known trail near his home. Accompanied by a friend, the two set off early in the morning on what was expected to be a routine outing.

However, Sheriff Todd Glander of Becker County reports that at 5:30 p.m., In Erie Township, his agency received a 911 report of a single snowmobile crash not far from the South Cotton Lake Road. The caller stated that Scott Fossum, the snowmobile’s 34-year-old driver, was not breathing.

Fossum was brought to St. Mary’s Hospital in Detroit Lakes by first responders, where he was later declared dead. Fossum reportedly lost control of his snowmobile and rolled into a ditch, according to Glander, although the crash is still being investigated. At the moment of the collision, Fossum was not wearing a helmet.

Beker County Sherriff’s Statement

“Early findings suggest that a number of factors may have been involved, even though the cause of the snowmobile crash is yet to be investigated. The state of the trail might have been a factor. Snowmobilers have more difficulty navigating the path because it hasn’t been groomed or maintained despite the recent snowfall”, they said.

How Did Scott Darrel Fossum Die? Cause of Death

A man from Fargo, Scott Darrel Fossum tragically passed away on Sunday, January 22nd, 2023, at the age of 34.

Scott reportedly lost control of his sled close to the intersection of County Highway 32 and South Cotton Lake Road northeast of Detroit Lakes, Minnesota, and rolled in the ditch, according to the sheriff’s office.

First responders discovered Fossum not breathing. He was transferred to the hospital in Detroit Lakes, where he passed away.
