The RHOBH cast, singer, Erika Jayne's Biography, Husband, Son, Net Worth

Erika Jayne is an American Television personality and singer whose several songs are listed number one on the U.S. Dance charts. Jayne has starred in the TV shows like Law & Order, Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, and The Young and the Restless. 

The entertainment personality was born as Erika Nay Chahoy on July 10, 1971, in Atlanta, Georgia, the United States of America. She is the daughter of a classically trained pianist, actress, Renee Chahoy. Her mother Renne and father separated when she was just nine months old. Additionally, Erika attended North Atlanta High School.
