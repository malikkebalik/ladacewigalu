Cody Rhodes: A Look at His Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Cody Rhodes has an estimated net worth of $2 million

The Career of Cody Rhodes

Cody Garrett Runnels, known professionally as Cody Rhodes, is a well-known professional wrestler. He has competed in several wrestling promotions, including Ohio Valley Wrestling and World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), achieving numerous titles and gaining recognition, all of which have contributed to his popularity and net worth. His wrestling career began in 2006.

Breaking Down Cody Rhodes’ Net Worth

Cody Rhodes’ father is the former wrestler and Hall of Famer Dusty Rhodes, who played a significant role in shaping Cody’s wrestling career. Cody’s journey started at Lassiter High School, where he achieved success in various wrestling competitions in his weight category. Instead of pursuing higher education at Penn State University, he chose to venture into professional wrestling.

He made his debut in Ohio Valley Wrestling under his real name and later adopted the ring name Cody Rhodes. Throughout his career, his net worth grew significantly, especially following his victories in different championships, including multiple tag team titles and an Intercontinental Championship title in the WWE, elevating his wealth.

After departing from the WWE, Cody continued his wrestling career in other promotions such as Evolve, Northeast Wrestling, and Ring Of Honor, further adding to his net worth.

Personal Life and Additional Information

Cody Rhodes has been married to Brandi Alexis Runnels since 2013, who is also a professional wrestler known by her ring name Brandi Rhodes.
