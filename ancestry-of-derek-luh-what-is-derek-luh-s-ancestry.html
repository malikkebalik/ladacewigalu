Career of Derek Luh

Derek Luh, a 31-year-old American hip-hop artist, songwriter, and actor from Valencia, Santa Clarita, California, embarked on his musical career in Yelm, Washington, in 2013.

His debut mixtape, Socks & Slides, released in 2014, garnered attention, leading to a signing with Mark Battle’s label, Fly America, and a tour with Machine Gun Kelly. His music, a fusion of hip-hop, pop, and rock, coupled with his vibrant live performances, gained him acclaim.

Luh’s debut album, For Your Consideration (2016), received critical acclaim, featuring the hit single “Lifestyle.” Subsequently, he released The Second Coming (2017), and LA Confidential (2019). His latest album, The Fortunate Few, was unveiled in 2022.

Additionally, Luh ventured into acting, making his debut in 2017 with a guest appearance on the Marvel show Runaways. He further showcased his acting prowess in various television shows, including All Rise and Shining Vale, with an upcoming role in Amazon Prime Video series The Boys spin-off.

Luh’s multifaceted talents, combined with his energetic live performances, captivating songs, and endearing personality, have propelled him as a rising star in the entertainment industry, poised for continued success.
