Tyron Bejay Facts

  • He was 5 years old when his family shifted to Bago.
  • Tyron Bejay started his career as a runway model in 2014.
  • He played the role of Khant Hte in the action-crime television series It was on Yesterday 2 in 2018.
  • In 2018, Tyron Bejay brought to life the character of Soe Htet (the main villain) in the drama film Kyway. He co-starred in the film alongside Hlwan Paing, Ei Chaw Po, Ye Aung, and Soe Myat Thuzar.
  • He co-starred as Tyron in the 2019 horror comedy film Kyauk Kyauk Kyauk 2: Journey to the Death, the sequel of the Kyauk Kyauk Kyauk film series.
  • In 2020, he was seen as Phay Khin (the young life of U Phay Khin) in the military drama television series Legends of Warriors.
  • Alongside Myint Myat, Htun Htun, Khin Hlaing, Soe Myat Thuzar, Ei Chaw Po, May Mi Ko Ko, and Hsaung Wutyee May, Tyron Bejay co-starred as Willian in the 2020 comedy drama film Players.
