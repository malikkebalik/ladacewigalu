Nadia Nakai Parent

It’s thought that Nadia Nakai’s parents are no longer married. Her mother is from Zimbabwe, and her father is from South Africa. While Nadia frequently discusses her mother in interviews, little is known about her father.

She spent her childhood with her mother, who had a huge impact on her musical preferences. Fans of Sade, Erykah Badu, Seal, and UB40 included Nadia’s mother. When The Money Back singer was a high school student in Kenya, her passion for rap music began to grow.

She was motivated to start rapping after meeting Nazizi, a female rapper. You could say that her mother laid the groundwork for her musical career, and Nazizi assisted her in discovering her calling.

Education

The real name of Nadia Nakai is a little confusing, I suppose. Nadia Nakai Dlamini is her legal name. She clearly profited from having a degree in media studies, marketing, and communication when she developed a brand that appeals to both men and women.

Nadia Nakai gathered a huge fan following on Instagram with more than a million followers prior to the release of her debut solo studio album.

Debut Album

The results of her labour are evident, and her supporters are thrilled. She became known as one of the top female MCs in the nation after the release of her debut album, Nadia Naked, in June 2019.

She teased the arrival of her eagerly awaited second album, Nadia Naked II, more than a year later. As predicted, Nadia Naked II eventually surfaced and peaked at number one in the early morning trends.

She includes some of the top rappers in Mzansi on the album, which is a certified banger and might be her best work to date. such as Vic Mensa, a Chicago-born American rapper, singer, and record producer, Lady Zamar, Tshego, and Kwesta.

Career

The early 2000s saw the arrival of Nadia on the music world. She made an appearance on the hip-hop programme Shiz Niz on e.tv, where she won the Mixtape 101 contest and made history as the first female rapper.

2013 saw the release of her debut song, “Like Me,” on the Psyfo-owned Sid Records label (now known as Sidwell). Before joining Family Tree, Nadia released two more songs through the previous label.

In 2017, Cassper assisted Nadia in releasing Naaa Mean, her biggest hit to date. She has worked with musicians like Ice Prince, Gemini Major, and Riky Rick.

Relationship

Due of their tight friendship, several people assumed Cassper Nyovest was Nadia Nakai’s boyfriend. She just referred to the rapper as “family” though. Most recently, Bandile Mbere of Major League was connected to Nadia.

Early in 2018 the couple acknowledged their relationship, however by early in 2019 they had broken up. Her sadness over the breakup served as the inspiration for one of the songs on her debut album, More Drugs.

After they broke up, Nadia put all of her focus into the record and it seems like she hasn’t had much time for romance. Many people assumed Nadia and Vic Mensa were dating as they started to appear very intimate.

Net Worth

It’s unclear how much Nadia Nakai is worth, despite some newspapers claiming that she has a net worth of roughly R11.8 million. However, Nadia is worth a good sum because to her endorsement deals with Redbat and Reebok as well as her job as a social media influencer and in the rap industry.
