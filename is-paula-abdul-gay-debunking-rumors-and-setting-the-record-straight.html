Is Paula Abdul Married?

At this time, Paula Abdul is not married to anyone. However, her dating history is quite intriguing; therefore, continue perusing to discover who she has previously dated.

Emilio Estevez

Paula Abdul, the “Opposites Attract” phenomenon, entered into matrimony with actor Emilio Estevez in the early 1990s, at the height of her career. Their romantic journey transpired in Paula’s meteoric rise to fame.

Nevertheless, the joy of matrimony only endured from 1992 to 1994. Alongside Paula, Emilio, who was renowned for his performances in landmark films such as The Breakfast Club and The Mighty Ducks, was thrust into the public eye.

Approximately three decades ago, Paula candidly disclosed the factors that contributed to their marital dissolution. It came down to a difficult realization for Emilio, as Paula described it:

“He found it extremely difficult to admit that he could not handle having children again.” It both devastated our hearts.”

Emilio, who had previously been married to model Carey Salley and had two children with her, Taylor Estevez (born in 1984) and Paloma Estevez (born in 1986), encountered intricacies that ultimately precipitated the dissolution of their partnership.

Brad Beckerman

After her divorce from Emilio, Paula married again, this time to businessman Brad Beckerman. Their marriage, however, was short-lived, ending in divorce two years later, in 1998.

Brad, a garment producer at the time, and Paula chose to divorce after only 16 months of marriage. The reason given was “irreconcilable differences,” and Paula went on with her life.

JT Torregiani

Paula Abdul’s on-again, off-again relationship with restaurateur JT Torregiani is one of the most well-known chapters in her romantic history. Their adventure began in 2007 but took a turn in 2014 when JT began dating Cheryl Burke from Dancing With The Stars.

JT told OK! magazine in the early days of their relationship that Paula’s maternal personality stood out, saying, “She is more nurturing than most women I know, let alone women I have dated.” Despite Paula’s busy schedule, he stressed that she always made time for him.

During an interview with People, Paula expressed her affection for JT, characterizing him as her “best friend.” “I’m in a good place in my heart,” she said back then.

By the end of 2020, stories appeared that Paula and JT were back in each other’s lives, reigniting their romance. Despite these previous relationships, Paula appears to be flying solo as of late, keeping the facts of her dating life under wraps. Whether she’s single or not, Paula Abdul continues to enchant audiences on and off the stage.
