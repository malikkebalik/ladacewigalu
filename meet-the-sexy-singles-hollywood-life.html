‘Too Hot To Handle’ season 4 premieres December 7 on Netflix. The cast includes contestants from around the world, including the United States, United Kingdom, Australia, and the Netherlands. The 10 singles will have to avoid temptation if they want to win the $100,000 grand prize. They cannot touch, kiss, or engage in any sexual activity. Anyone who breaks those rules is removed from the overall prize pot. This season filmed in Turks and Caicos.
