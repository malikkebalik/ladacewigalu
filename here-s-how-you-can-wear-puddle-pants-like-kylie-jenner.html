Heres How You Can Wear Puddle Pants Like Kylie Jenner

Publish date: 2024-07-05

ICYMI, Kylie Jenner has joined the ranks of celebrities like Bella Hadid and Kate Middleton to popularise pants that puddle around the feet, AKA Puddle Pants.

This bizarre-yet-captivating trend was also spotted on F/W runways of designers like Peter Do and Balenciaga and has now trickled down to street style.

Jenner was spotted wearing the trend during a recent trip to New York City. The trend has been slowly gaining traction, along with many other style trends from the early aughts, mainly because of how comfortable, if not entirely practical, it is.

The beauty mogul balanced the proportions of her baggy jeans by pairing them with a cropped button-up, a lesson you can set in stone if you’re trying out the look for yourself. Go for a pair of platform sneakers or chunky loafers to ensure that the bottoms of your bottoms do not serve as floor sweepers when you’re out on the town. Or you can skip the height and go full puddle with the Puddle Pants and opt for flats. You only live once, right?
