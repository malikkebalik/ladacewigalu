Abigail Faye Cowen is an American actress, model, and singer, best known for her role of Dorcas in the TV series Chilling Adventures of Sabrina starting in 2018. Other lead roles in the series were played by Kiernan Shipka, Ross Lynch, Lucy Davis, Chance Perdomo, Jaz Sinclair, Tati Gabrielle, Adeline Rudolph, Richard Coyle, Miranda Otto, and Lachlan Watson. Abigail began her career with a guest role of Brooklyn in TV series Red Band Society in 2014, and after a 3-year break, appeared in 2 episodes of Stranger Things (2017) as Vicki.

Between 2017 and 2018, she starred as Mia Tanner in the series Wisdom of the Crowd, and in 2018 played Eliza Hunter in 4 episodes of The Fosters. As a model, Abigail is signed to MC2 Model Management and IMG Models. Despite not being a recording artist, Abigail became well-known for song covers that she posted via social media, often accompanied by an acoustic guitar played by her or her friends. She has amassed an online fanbase consisting of more than 600k followers on Instagram.
