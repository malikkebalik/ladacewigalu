Publish date: 2024-08-01

See photos of Naira Marley’s new house at Lekki

Popular Nigeria musician Azeez Fashola who is known by his professional name Naira Marley has acquired a new house in Lekki, Lagos Island.

Naira Marley took to his official Instagram stories to post pictures of the new house to show off to his controversial fans known as Marlians.

It was in the post, Naira Marley stated that the new house is his fifth and where the house is located.

With the majority of Nigerians which include his Marlians are struggling with the current economic climate created by the deadly Coronavirus, he intends to create a soup kitchen to cater to their needs.

According to Naira Marley, the kitchen is non-profit and the less privileged can come to eat and get items they need.
