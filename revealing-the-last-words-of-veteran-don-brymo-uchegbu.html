Revealing the Last Words of Veteran Don Brymo Uchegbu

Publish date: 2024-08-15

The final words of veteran actor, Don Brymo Uchegbu have been finally revealed.

Kemi Filani reported minutes ago that Nollywood has been thrown into mourning yet again as they lose one of their veterans, Don Brymo Uchegbu.

The movie star, who days back was seen dancing heartily in a viral video, was said to have died at 56 on Thursday morning, June 15th.

According to reports online, Don Brymo collapsed in his hotel room in Ahoada, Rivers State, after returning from a movie location.

He was reportedly rushed to an undisclosed hospital where he was diagnosed with partial stroke and a high sugar level.

He was subsequently discharged from the hospital in Port Harcourt and moved to a hospital in Anambra for further treatment, unfortunately, the actor couldn’t survive.

A report now making rounds revealed the actor’s final words before he gave up the ghost.

An insider said his last words were, “Odogwu has turned to vegetable”. He made this statement in his dialect, Igbo, before taking his last breath.

Junior Pope confirms Don Brymo’s death

Confirming the news of his death, actor, Junior Pope took to his Instagram page to mourn him.

He noted how all who walk on Earth must die, however, true heroes don’t die.

Junior Pope promised that the veteran would forever remain in their memories.

Saint Obi is dead

Last month, Nollywood lost one of its beloved veterans, Obinna Nwafor better known as Saint Obi.

The actor was said to have died on Sunday, May 7th in the home of one of his siblings, in Jos, after a prolonged illness.

According to reports, the disagreement between his siblings was the reason why his death took almost a week to be made official.

Further details surrounding his death had been kept under wraps. The veteran was aged 57 years and has three kids who he shares with estranged, Linda Amobi.

The estranged couple were engaged in a bitter divorce custody battle before his untimely death.
