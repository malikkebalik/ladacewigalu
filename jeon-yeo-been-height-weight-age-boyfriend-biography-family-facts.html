Jeon Yeo-been is a South Korean actor who rose to fame with her role as Young-hee in the 2018 mystery drama film, After My Death. This role won her multiple accolades including the 19th Busan Film Critics Award, 28th Buil Film Award, CGV Art House Special Award, 56th Grand Bell Award, 24th Chunsa Film Art Award, and 10th Film Award of the Year. Moreover, Jeon Yeo-been has also been cast in other films and shows like The Running Actress, Secret Zoo, Forbidden Dream, Save Me, Merry Christmas Mr. Mo, Illang: The Wolf Brigade, Be Melodramatic, Vincenzo, Night in Paradise, The Best Director, and Live.

Jeon Yeo-been Facts

  • She started acting at the age of 25.
  • Jeon Yeo-been played the character named Yeo-been in the 2016 drama film, Write or Dance, which is the story of an aspiring novelist who experiences special meetings with different individuals on a particular day of every month. For this performance, she won the award for “Best New Actress” at the Marie Claire Film Festival.
  • She was cast as Lee Eun-jung, a documentary director who is the CEO and only employee of her company, in the romantic comedy television series, Be Melodramatic, in 2019. This role got Jeon Yeo-been nominated for a 56th Baeksang Arts Award in the “Best New Actress (TV)” category.
