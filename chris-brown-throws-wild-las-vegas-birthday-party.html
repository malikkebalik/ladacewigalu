Chris Brown Throws Wild Las Vegas Birthday Party

Publish date: 2024-09-03

It was day two of Chris' birthday celebrations on May 4, as the R&B singer descended upon Sin City for yet another party. Rihanna didn't show up, but Chris still seemed to have a great time. Keep reading for more details. Chris Brown is definitely going to crash for a few days after this weekend. The singer attended his second birthday party of the weekend on May 4, this time at 1 Oak in Las Vegas, Nev.

Chris Brown Parties Hard In Las Vegas

It was a night jam-packed with fun. Before hitting up his party at the swanky nightclub, Chris attended the Floyd Mayweather Jr.–Robert Guerrero boxing match at the MGM Grand Garden Arena. The fight was a huge spectacle and the king of the welterweights, Floyd Mayweather, ended up winning in an unanimous decision after 12 rounds of fighting.

Chris and his crew then got their party on at the club. The star sipped champagne and danced all night long, ringing in his May 5 birthday the right way.

He actually might have overdone it: early in the morning on May 5 Chris tweeted, “Do too wasted at the party. Threw up crazy!! Lol!” Well, if there’s one day when you’re allowed to puke, it’s your birthday, right?

Chris Brown: Missing Rihanna On His Birthday?

Chris has had an awesome birthday weekend, he’s just missing one thing — Rihanna. The Unapologetic singer has been marooned in the New York City area because of scheduled concerts on her “Diamonds” tour.

HollywoodLife.com reported EXCLUSIVELY that Chris “wished she could be with him on his birthday, just like he was with her,” according to a source. Rihanna surely would have treated Breezy like a king if she was able to celebrate with him. But the source assures us that the couple will still celebrate together when their schedules open up: “They’ll make it up to each other and it will go down,” the insider says.
