Woodz's Announced Last Concert Before Enlistment

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Before he enlists in the military, Woodz is going to make some unforgettable memories with his fans. According to EDAM Entertainment, Woodz’s agency, “Woodz will hold the final concert of his world tour ‘OO-LI’ on January 19th next year at the Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Seoul.” With the help of his devoted fan base, Mooz, he will celebrate the end of the ‘OO-LI’ project at this performance.

With the release of the ‘OO-LI’ album in April and the subsequent ‘OO-LI And.’ encore world tour, the tour is coming to a close with this concert. It represents an occasion to celebrate in a more solemn setting with all those who have contributed to this voyage.

The agency claims that Woodz will be able to showcase his progress from his tour by performing at a significantly larger venue this time around.

Up earlier on the 18th, Woodz dropped the digital single ‘Amnesia.’ The title track was a huge hit, topping the iTunes Chart in Thailand and ranking 35th on the Worldwide iTunes Chart (as of the release date). It also did well on other major music sites.
